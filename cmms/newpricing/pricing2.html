Frequently asked questions

Can I trial the software?

Absolutely. You can test the Professional version of Fiix at your own pace through ourfree touror participate in one of ourlive product demos.

What is the free tour?

We’ve put together a live demo of the Professional version of Fiixso that you can take a free tour on your own time.You can access it from thefree tour page.

Can I see a product demo?

You sure can. Pleasecontact usand we’ll arrange to show you the product at a time that makes sense for you.

Can I attend an online product webinar?

Of course.We run free 45 minute live product demo webinars every week day at 1PM Eastern Time. You can sign-up to joinhere. Once you’ve signed up, you’ll get an email with all the details. Its a great place to get all your questions answered.

How flexible are your plans?

Fiixplans are subscription based. That means you have complete flexibility to chose a month-to-month subscription or annual subscription plan. Feel free tocontact usif you have additional questions.

Are there any additional setup or hardware fees?

Absolutely not. The price you see is the price you pay. There are no additional setup or hardwarefees associated with your plan.

What is a user?

A user is any named person who logs into Fiix. We recommend that each technician, admin, or manager have their own user license.Add as many users as you want to your plan at any time.

Do you offer volume-based pricing?

We believe in being completely flexible at Fiix which is why we’re happy to offer our Performance pricing plan to extend Fiix to more teams, to more users, for less.Feel free tocontact uswith your wish list and we’ll do what we can to make it happen.

What level of support comes with my subscription plan?

All plans receive free product upgrades, in-application video tutorials, and access to ourhelp centre. All of our subscription plans come with email and phone support during regular business hours.

Will you increase my subscription prices?

We will not increase your subscription price during the terms of your contract. Pricing may be reassessed at renewal to reflect more value being added to your system.

Does Fiix integrate with any other applications?

We sure do. We have manypre-built apps and integrationsthat are available to you right out of the box.Have other integrations in mind? We build customfunctionality at a breakneck pace and we’d love to work with you to build the customsolution that you need.Get in touch today.

How do you keep data private and secure?

We use multi-factor authentication, IP protection, 256 bit SSL encryption, intrusion detection, minimum password strength algorithms, CAPTCHA protection on failed login for DOS threats, multi-server/geography daily back-ups and much more. You get the picture, we take security very seriously. You can find out more about oursecurity measures here.
