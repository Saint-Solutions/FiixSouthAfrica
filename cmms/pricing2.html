Frequently asked questions


Can I trial the software?

Absolutely. You can test the Professional version of Fiix at your own pace through our free tour or participate in one of our live product demos.


What is the free tour?

Weve put together a live demo of the Professional version of Fiix so that you can take a free tour on your own time. You can access it from the free tour page.


Can I see a product demo?

You sure can. Please contact us and well arrange to show you the product at a time that makes sense for you.


Can I attend an online product webinar?

Of course. We run free 45 minute live product demo webinars every week day at 1PM Eastern Time. You can sign-up to join here. Once youve signed up, youll get an email with all the details. Its a great place to get all your questions answered.


How flexible are your plans?

Fiix plans are subscription based. That means you have complete flexibility to chose a month-to-month subscription or annual subscription plan. Feel free to contact us if you have additional questions.


Are there any additional setup or hardware fees?

Absolutely not. The price you see is the price you pay. There are no additional setup or hardware fees associated with your plan.


What is a user?

A user is any named person who logs into Fiix. We recommend that each technician, admin, or manager have their own user license. Add as many users as you want to your plan at any time.


Do you offer volume based pricing?

We believe in being completely flexible at Fiix which is why were happy to offer our Performance pricing plan to extend Fiix to more teams, to more users, for less. Feel free to contact us with your wish list and well do what we can to make it happen.


What level of support comes with my subscription plan?

All plans receive free product upgrades, in-application video tutorials, and access to our help centre. All of our subscription plans come with email and phone support during regular business hours.


Will you increase my subscriptions prices?

We will not increase your subscription price during the terms of your contract. Pricing may be reassessed at renewal to reflect more value being added to your system.


Does Fiix integrate with any other applications?

We sure do. We have many pre-built apps and integrations that are available to you right out of the box. Have other integrations in mind? We build custom functionality at a breakneck pace and wed love to work with you to build the custom solution that you need. Get in touch today.


How do you keep data private and secure?

We use multi-factor authentication, IP protection, 256 bit SSL encryption, intrusion detection, minimum password strength algorithms, CAPTCHA protection on failed login for DOS threats, multi-server/geography daily back-ups and much more. You get the picture, we take security very seriously.
