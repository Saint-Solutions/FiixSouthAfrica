Benefits of using maintenance software for wastewater management

Fiix’s wastewater asset management software helps maintenance teams drive productivity by allowing them to:

  • Track documentation and audit logs to meet pollutant discharge and EPA regulations
  • Increase uptime on primary and secondary treatment assets
  • Ensure compliance and audit preparedness for state and federal reviews
  • Automatically trigger work orders with an intuitive meter reading system
  • Assign users to specific work orders so work is completed quicker
