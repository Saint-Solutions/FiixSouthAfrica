Benefits of using food manufacturing maintenance software

Fiix’s food manufacturing software helps maintenance teams drive productivity by allowing them to:

  • Prepare for audits, such as the Global Food Safety Initiative (GFSI), Good Manufacturing Practices (GMP), and ISO 22000 Audit
  • Provide detailed documentation and audit logs that show the steps taken to comply with health and safety legislation
  • Organize and view machines, systems and buildings in an intuitive hierarchy
  • Easily access the work order history of any asset
  • Assign maintenance managers to specific buildings, machines, production lines, systems and assets
  • Schedule preventive maintenance on high-volume and high-risk machinery
  • Have machines and equipment at all facilities automatically trigger work orders based on usage
