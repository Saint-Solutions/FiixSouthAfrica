Benefits of using equipment maintenance software

Using equipment maintenance software can help improves your organization by allowing you to:

  • Organize and view your equipment in an intuitive hierarchy
  • Easy access to work order history for any asset
  • Assign maintenance managers to specific buildings and assets
  • Run preventive maintenance and scheduled maintenance
  • Create standard operating procedures for maintenance activities
  • Send email alerts to appropriate people when work is assigned
  • Track all warranties, service contracts and manuals in one place
