Benefits of industrial maintenance software

Fiix’s industrial maintenance software helps maintenance teams drive productivity by allowing them to:

  • Ensure operational readiness of all safety equipment
  • View current and upcoming maintenance work orders
  • Organize facilities and equipment into a hierarchy
  • Schedule preventive maintenance on high-risk machines and assets
  • Improve industrial maintenance planning and scheduling to reduce the likelihood of accidents and reduce downtime
  • Meet OHS standards for industrial hygiene, protection, and safety
